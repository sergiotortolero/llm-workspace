# Project Context — <Project Name>

> Deep context for a single project. Copy this file, fill it in (Spanish or English),
> and drop it in `../inbox/` or directly in the project's `docs/`. This is richer than
> `CLAUDE.md` (which holds only rules) — here goes the "why" and the background.

## 1. Vision (one paragraph)
What is this product and why does it exist?

## 2. Users / customers
Who uses it? Internal? External clients? White-label for others?

## 3. Current status
What exists today? What works? What's broken or missing?

## 4. Goal & deadlines
What does "done" or "success" look like? Any dates?

## 5. Stack & constraints
Known tech, hard constraints (budget, hosting, must-use tools), things you can't change.

## 6. Key decisions made
Decisions already taken (and why) — so we don't relitigate them.

## 7. Blockers / open questions
What's stuck or undecided right now.

## 8. Links & resources
Repos, Figma, NotebookLM notebooks, Drive folders, dashboards, tickets, docs.
(Links only — no passwords/tokens.)

## 9. Who's involved
Team, stakeholders, who decides, who you report to.
